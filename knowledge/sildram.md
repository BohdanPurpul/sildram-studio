# Sildram Studio

Sildram Studio creates clear digital solutions for businesses and private clients. The studio focuses on practical AI assistants, Telegram bots, CRM integrations, business automation, and modern websites.

## Main Directions

- AI assistants for business and personal scenarios.
- AI consultants for websites.
- Telegram bots.
- Business process automation.
- Request processing automation.
- CRM integrations and internal workflows.
- Website development.
- Personal AI assistants.
- AI Store Assistant as a demo and portfolio product.

## Who It Is For

Sildram Studio solutions are suitable for small and medium businesses, online stores, service companies, experts, private clients, and projects that need to automate requests, consultations, repetitive work, or client communication.

## AI Assistants

AI assistants can answer common questions, collect client details, ask clarifying questions, and prepare a structured request for the team. They can work in Telegram, on a website, or inside internal company processes.

## AI Consultants For Websites

An AI consultant on a website helps visitors understand services, choose the next step, ask clarifying questions, and submit a request. It can help reduce repetitive manager replies and guide visitors toward the contact form.

## Telegram Bots

A Telegram bot can receive requests, ask clarifying questions, collect contact information, answer common questions, notify the team, and pass data to a manager or CRM. Telegram bots are useful when clients contact the business through messengers and requests need to be organized.

## CRM Integrations

CRM integrations help transfer requests into CRM without manual copying. They can reduce chaos in client communication, help control statuses and responsible managers, and make request handling easier to track.

## Business Automation

Business automation focuses on reducing repetitive manual work, organizing requests, improving response speed, and connecting website forms, Telegram, WhatsApp, CRM, and internal workflows where needed.

## Website Development

Sildram Studio creates landing pages, corporate websites, and pages with clear structure, request forms, Telegram integration, AI consultant readiness, mobile adaptation, and basic SEO-friendly structure. A website should explain the offer, build trust, and help receive requests.

## Personal AI Assistants

Personal AI assistants can support planning, learning, consulting, tutoring, Telegram-based reminders, and individual productivity scenarios. They are built around the user's workflow and practical tasks.

## AI Store Assistant

AI Store Assistant is a demo portfolio solution for online stores. It demonstrates how an AI consultant can answer product questions, help with catalog navigation, cart and order context, demo payment flow, and hand off information to a manager.

## Process Of Work

The work process is simple: understand the task, suggest a clear plan, discuss the format and scope, create the first understandable version, test it, launch it, and help after launch.

## Contacts

Contacts are available on the Contacts page. The public channels shown on the site include Telegram, Instagram, GitHub, Email, Phone, WhatsApp, Facebook, and Gmail. If a visitor wants to discuss a project, they should be guided to the request form on the Contacts page.

## Pricing

Sildram Studio does not publish fixed prices on the site. Cost depends on required functionality, integrations, and project complexity. The assistant must not invent prices, price ranges, deadlines, guarantees, fake cases, statistics, ROI, or discounts.

## What Sildram Studio Does Not Do

Sildram Studio does not promise guaranteed sales or profit. It does not promise mass cold outreach, unsolicited messaging, scraping contacts, or spam. The assistant should not give legal, medical, financial, or unrelated technical advice.
